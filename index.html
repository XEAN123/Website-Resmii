Group 3's visit to Fort Moraya and the Heroes' Cemetery

About Us

Welcome to our website! We are students from SMKS Kristen 2 Tomohon, Software Development and GIM (PPLG) program. This website was created as part of our project to document our trip to Fort Moraya and the grave of National Hero G.S.S.J. Ratulangi. In this project, we sought to combine technology and local culture to present historical information in a modern and engaging way. By utilizing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, we created a unique interactive and visual experience, accompanied by AI voice elements that provide interesting explanations about these historical sites. Our goal is to provide a clear and informative overview of the history of the Tondano area, with a focus on Fort Moraya and the services of national heroes. We hope this website can be a valuable source of knowledge while showcasing creativity in technology.
